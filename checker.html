Red Flags to Watch

Suspicious Domain Extensions
Scam sites use .tk, .ml, .xyz, .ga instead of .com or .in. Real banks never use these.
Missing HTTPS Padlock
No padlock in the address bar means the site is not secure. Never enter your password.
Misspelled Bank Names
Scammers use sbi-secure.com or st4tebank.com. Always check spelling character by character.
Urgency + Reward Language
"Claim your prize now!" or "Your account will be blocked!" — these are manipulation tactics.
